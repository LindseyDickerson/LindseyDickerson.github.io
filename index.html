About Me

After spending 11 years in the Property & Casualty industry, I was ready for a change. I enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy to learn JavaScript, and am seeking to be a part of a team environment that values continuing education and diversity in the workplace.
I have a strong customer focused background, and a desire to continually learn. I am a lifelong Hoosier - I graduated from Pike High School and went on to attend Butler University, graduating in 2006.
I enjoy cooking, playing the flute, and volunteering. I became involved with Girl Scouts of Central Indiana in 2016, and have thoroughly enjoyed my time with the troop. It has taught me a lot about myself, and even helped me learn and grow. I volunteer at The Shalom House serving meals to the community of Boone County and other activities to help combat food insecurity.
